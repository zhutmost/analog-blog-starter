Welcome to my blog.

I am Haozhe Zhu (朱浩哲).

Currently, I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor (助理教授、博士生导师) at [Frontier Institute of Chip & System, Fudan University](https://fics.fudan.edu.cn).

Most of my works lie in an overlap of integrated circuits and artificial intelligence.

## Education & Work Experiences

- 2025.6-Present, Assistant Professor, Fudan University.
- 2022.6-2025.5, Post-doctoral Researcher, Fudan University, with [Prof. Qi Liu](https://fics.fudan.edu.cn/8e/48/c22619a429640/page.htm).
- 2017.9-2022.6, Ph.D. in Microelectronics and Solid-state Electronics, Fudan University, with [Prof. Xiaoyang Zeng](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/author/37288001700).
- 2013.9-2017.6, B.Eng. in Microelectronics Science & Engineering, Fudan University.

## Academic Services

- Executive Committee Member - [CCF Technical Committee of Computer Engineering and Techniques](https://www.ccf.org.cn/Chapters/TC/TC_Listing/TCCET) (CCF计算机工程工艺专委) - Since 2025.
- Publicity Chair - [3rd Integrated Chips & Chiplets Conference (ICCC)](https://2025.iccconf.cn/) (第三届集成芯片与芯粒大会) - 2025.

I have also served as a reviewer for many conferences and journals, including but not limited to: JETCAS, TCAS-1, TCAS-2, TVLSI, OJ-SSCS, ...
